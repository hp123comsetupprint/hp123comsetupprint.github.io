Signs Your Printer Needs Repair

If you notice any of the following issues, it might be time for professional repair:

  • Printer not powering on or frequent shutdowns.
  • Unusual noises during printing.
  • Frequent paper jams or multiple sheets being pulled in.
  • Poor print quality with streaks, spots, or faded text.
  • Connectivity issues or failure to recognize devices.
  • Ink or toner leakage causing smudged prints.
