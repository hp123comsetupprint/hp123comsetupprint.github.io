When Should You Replace Ink or Toner?

It’s time to replace your ink or toner if you notice any of the following:

  • Faded or streaky prints.
  • Low ink or toner warnings on your printer.
  • Blurry or uneven print quality.
  • Printer stops printing due to empty cartridges.
